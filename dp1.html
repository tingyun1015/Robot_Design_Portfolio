01
Motivation & Overview

A creative robot designed to perform to music, capturing the energetic presence of a pop idol.

Project Goals
  • Create a robot that can move and play music.
  • Strengthen hands-on making skills.
  • Combine Arduino control with handcrafted lighting and sound effects.
  • Present feedback through appearance and sound to boost creativity.
Features
  • Simple Arduino circuit.
  • Easy for beginners to assemble and program.
  • Includes sound, movement, and light effects.

Results & Reflection

A working idol robot that moves, lights up and plays music — and a lot learned about bridging electronics and craft.

Final Outcome
  • Built an Arduino robot
  • Added music and lights
  • Robot can move and turn
  • Combined tech and design
Learning Gains
  • Basic Arduino coding
  • Circuit & component integration
  • Handmade design skills
Challenges
  • Combining the look with the electronics
  • Adjusting balance and stability
  • Many rounds of testing lights & movement
Future Directions
  • Add more light modes

  • Add more interactive features
