Design Process

From a clear visitor problem to a consistent guiding personality — defined, designed, and refined across real park scenarios.

01

Define

Identified the challenge visitors face when planning a one-day Disney park trip, and explored how an AI assistant could support decision-making before and during the visit.

02

Design

Designed Maya as a fairy guide character that combines the friendliness and professionalism of a Disney Cast Member with a sense of companionship.

03

Refine

Tested Maya across various visitor scenarios and continuously refined her responses, personality, and conversation scope for a consistent guiding experience.
